Just Desserts

Gunch Studios
Nick Butti, Jordan Colburn, Zach Nadeau

Select your commander and play a series of tower defense levels as you progress through a randomly generated map. Find shops to unlock upgrades for your towers and commander, and fight mini bosses for bonus rewards to prepare for Count Cakeula

StoutHearted

Free Shavacado
Aden Berthiaume, Chris Pacheco, Greg Jones Jr

StoutHearted is a retro-inspired, Dungeon Crawler game where you play as a boy who witnessed Death take your father. Unable to accept this, you make your way through levels to confront Death and get your dad back.

AUTO-MATIC

39 Studios
Andrew Hill, Dominic Pacheco, Keegan Sullivan

A pilot on a space station must use his trusty mech to defeat hostile robots and upgrade his mech before facing the rogue AI that took over his home.

Starship Destroyer

North Star Studios
alex Helm, Derek Pena, Kyle Sangillo, Jake Souza

Starship Destroyer is a 3D Space Battle Simulator, It Has an Earth fighter pilot It is your duty to defend Earth against the alien hostile attack. Destroying Ships lets you collect scrap to upgrade your ship and your defense fleet.
